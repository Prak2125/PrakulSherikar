About

Engineer.
Racer. Builder.

I'm Prakul, a rising junior at UT Austin studying Mechanical Engineering (GPA 3.74). My work sits at the intersection of structural design, FEA simulation, and hands-on fabrication — most visibly through Longhorn Baja SAE, where I lead front upright design for our off-road race vehicle.

Outside the lab, I'm a flagging marshal at COTA for FIA WEC and Trans Am events, a sim racing competitor, and a hobby roboticist through IEEE RAS. I'm drawn to problems where weight, load, and geometry all have to come together precisely.

I'm actively looking for internships and co-ops in the Seattle area — including part-time roles that work alongside the school year. Long-term I'm aiming for graduate school in mechanical or aerospace engineering.
